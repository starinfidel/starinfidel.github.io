amoebaRL v0.1

The goal of the game is to eat weaker amoebas while running away from stronger ones.

  • when eating weaker amoeba you gain a level;
  • when stronger amoeba chews you, you die;
  • you have a 66% chance of growing when eating amoeba of the same level;
  • amoebas of level 9 are always stronger than you
  • you win by gaining level 10.
